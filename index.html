ABOUT DR REBECCA U. ETIUBON

Dr. Rebecca U. Etiubon is a highly accomplished academic and educator in the field of chemistry and science education. She holds a Bachelor of Science degree with honors in Chemistry, a Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Education), a Master's degree in Analytical Chemistry, and a Ph.D. in Chemistry Education. She is also a member of the Teacher Registration Council of Nigeria (mTRCN).

Dr. Etiubon is affiliated with the Department of Science Education within the Faculty of Education at the University of Uyo in Uyo, Nigeria. Her academic career has been dedicated to promoting excellence in science education and equipping future educators with the necessary knowledge and skills.

With her background in chemistry and expertise in science education, Dr. Etiubon has conducted research and contributed to the advancement of teaching methodologies and curriculum development in her field. Her work focuses on improving the quality of science education at various levels, ensuring that students receive a solid foundation in scientific principles and critical thinking.
